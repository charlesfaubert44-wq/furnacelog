## Brainstorming Framework

### 1. **AI & Machine Learning Features**

**Prompt Questions:**
- How could AI predict system failures before they happen?
- What patterns could machine learning detect in maintenance data?
- How could we use computer vision to analyze photos of systems?
- Could AI optimize maintenance schedules based on usage patterns?
- How could natural language processing help with maintenance notes?

**Ideas to Explore:**
- **Predictive Maintenance AI:**
  - Analyze maintenance history patterns
  - Predict "Time to Failure" for each system
  - Alert: "Your furnace typically fails after 18 months without filter change - you're at 16 months"
  - Machine learning from thousands of homes to predict issues

- **Photo Analysis AI:**
  - Auto-detect rust, corrosion, leaks from uploaded photos
  - "Your water heater shows signs of sediment buildup (82% confidence)"
  - Before/after comparison with degradation scoring
  - Auto-tag photos with detected issues

- **Smart Recommendations Engine:**
  - Learn from your maintenance patterns
  - Personalized schedules based on your actual usage
  - "You check your furnace filter every 6 weeks, not 3 months like others"
  - Optimize for cost vs. longevity based on preferences

- **Chatbot Maintenance Assistant:**
  - "What's wrong with my furnace?" → AI diagnosis from symptoms
  - Voice logging: "Log maintenance - changed furnace filter today"
  - Conversational help: "How do I bleed my radiators?"

## Evaluation Criteria for New Features

Rate each idea (1-10) on:

1. **User Value** - How much does this improve homeowner's life?
2. **Uniqueness** - Is this a differentiator?
3. **Feasibility** - Can we actually build this?
4. **Revenue Potential** - Could this be monetized?
5. **Northern Focus** - Does this serve our niche exceptionally well?
6. **Scalability** - Will this work for 10 users? 10,000 users?
7. **Wow Factor** - Will users tell friends about this?

**Scoring:**
- 60-70: Must-have feature, build ASAP
- 50-59: Strong feature, prioritize
- 40-49: Good feature, consider
- Below 40: Interesting but not critical
